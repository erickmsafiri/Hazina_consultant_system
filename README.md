Hazina Consultancy - Business & Tourism Management System
Hazina Consultancy is a complete web-based management system built with PHP, MySQL, JavaScript, and Bootstrap for consulting firms, training centers, and tourism businesses in Tanzania. The system features a professional frontend website with service listings, training courses, and tourism packages, plus a full admin dashboard for managing payments (M-Pesa & Airtel Money integration), tracking attendance, generating PDF/Excel reports, and monitoring revenue. It includes an AI-powered chatbot "KNOWLEDGE IS WEALTH AI" that answers customer questions 24/7, a responsive mobile-friendly design with bottom navigation, and secure authentication. Perfect for businesses offering research consultation, business services, professional training, and tourism planning.
